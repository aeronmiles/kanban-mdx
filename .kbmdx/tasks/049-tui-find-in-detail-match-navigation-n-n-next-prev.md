Go TUI detail find has full match navigation: n/f to jump to next match, N/F to jump to previous match, ctrl+n/ctrl+p to navigate while still typing in the input. Matches are highlighted in the rendered markdown. kanban-mdx detail view has ctrl+f to open search but lacks match-by-match navigation — n/f are mapped to quick-move instead.
